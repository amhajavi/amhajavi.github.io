Amirhossein Hajavi

Ph.D Candidate @ Queen's University

Hello there! I'm Amirhossein but you can call me Amir. Currently I'm in my 4th year of my Ph.D studies at the ECE department of Queen's University. I am a machine learning enthusiast with an unhealthy obsession with deep learning. My focus during Ph.D was on audio signal processing using deep learning solutions specially speaker representation learning. However, I also get excited when looking into interesting deep learning methods used in other fields. That being said, I'm planning to finish my Ph.D by the end of this summer (Aug 2022) and can't wait to pursue interesting things in deep learning whereever it might be.
